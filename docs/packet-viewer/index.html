assay Packet Viewer v0

Inspect a signed review packet.

Agents and workflows produce work. Assay turns that work into packets a reviewer can inspect, verify, and challenge.

Product rule: provenance says where an artifact came from. Assay says what a reviewer may and may not rely on. Static demo. No backend. No account.
