# Lead Selection Rubric

Internal. 10 names max. Ranked by likelihood of feeling eval drift
pain, speed to reply, and warmth of connection.

---

## Target profile

**Primary:** Eval owner / ML platform lead at an AI product company.
5-50 person eng team. Running structured LLM-as-judge evaluations.
Shipping model improvements based on eval scores.

**Signals that they feel the pain:**
- Talks publicly about eval reliability, benchmark quality, or judge calibration
- Has published eval methodology or benchmark results
- Works at a company where eval scores drive model promotion, feature launch, or budget decisions
- Has mentioned regression testing, A/B testing of prompts, or model version pinning
- Uses or builds on frameworks like Braintrust, Langfuse, or custom eval harnesses
- Has complained about eval noise, non-reproducibility, or "improvements that didn't replicate"

**Signals they'd reply fast:**
- Active on Twitter/X, LinkedIn, or relevant Discord/Slack communities
- Engages with AI tooling or eval content
- Has a public email or DM-open profile
- You have a warm connection (shared community, mutual follow, prior interaction)

## Scoring (1-5 per dimension)

| Dimension | 1 (weak) | 5 (strong) |
|-----------|----------|------------|
| **Eval pain** | Uses evals casually, no structured pipeline | Runs daily judge evals, ships decisions from scores |
| **Warmth** | Cold stranger, no connection | Direct mutual, prior conversation |
| **Speed** | Low online presence, unlikely to see message | Active, responsive, DMs open |
| **Authority** | IC without budget control | Team lead / manager who can authorize a pilot |
| **Company fit** | Research lab / pre-product | AI product company shipping to users |

**Minimum threshold:** Total score >= 15 out of 25 to be worth a first message.

## What NOT to optimize for

- Do not select for "most impressive company name"
- Do not select for "biggest team" (big teams move slowly)
- Do not select for "most likely to be impressed" (you need honest signal, not flattery)
- Do not select for compliance/risk buyers yet (that's lane 2, not lane 1)

## After the first 3 sends

Record:
- Did they reply? (Y/N/pending)
- What did they ask first?
- What confused them?
- Did the pain resonate or did they redirect to a different pain?
- Would they take the diagnostic? (Y/N/maybe)

That data shapes whether the wedge is right or needs adjustment.
