How to verify

How can an independent reviewer validate this passport?

assay passport verify ./passport.json
  • passport_id matches SHA-256 of passport body (content-addressed identity)
  • Ed25519 signature is valid (if signed)
  • valid_until has not passed
  • Referenced proof pack passes integrity verification
  • No active challenge, supersession, or revocation receipts in passport directory

How to challenge

How is this artifact disputed, updated, or revoked?

assay passport challenge ./passport.json --reason "<your reason>"
  1. A timestamped challenge receipt is created in the passport directory.
  2. Passport status becomes CHALLENGED on next verification.
  3. The challenge reason and timestamp are immutably recorded.
  4. The issuer can respond by superseding (issuing a new passport that addresses the challenge) or revoking.
